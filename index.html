Overview
Vegetarian Gardens Inc. is the premier supplier of health food products in Guyana, South America. Our product line includes beverages and food products from world renowned brands such as Kellogg's, Vitasoy, Ginseng Up USA, Bragg, Mori-Nu, etc. After more than 15 years in the industry, our private brand Vita-Burga Chunks is the number one soy meat on the Guyanese market.
We practice what we preach, healthy eating.
